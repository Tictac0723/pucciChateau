Our Mission: To treat every dog with love and respect. To provide a healthy, happy, and loving experience for every dog we have the privilege of being trusted to care for.

Our services

Dog DayCare

Here your pup receives a safe, fun, stimulating and structured environment to play. Pups are off-leash and cage free with other dogs who have passed our temperament evaluation, under the supervision of trained staff.

Dog Boarding

We have an open play environment with indoor & outdoor play areas, plenty of water, experienced & loving staff, state-of-the-art facility designed with dogs’ needs in mind, a comfortable climate controlled facility, bedding and toys are sanitized daily, and 24 hour dog cams.

Salon Grooming

Sanitation Practices, Skin & Hair Education as not every dog cannot use the same product in their hair. We offer a stress-free environment during grooming. We keep Pet Parents well-informed about how their furbaby is doing during the grooming process.

Dog Training

Our trainer (Bj Land with Lone Star Schutzhund) designs a program specifically to meet the goals and needs of you and your pup! Our 5 week training Our training programs are individual rather than group sessions.
